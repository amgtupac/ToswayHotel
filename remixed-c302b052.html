Section 04
04

Design Approach

For Tosway Hotel, we will apply a refined, hospitality-focused design language — elegant without being overcomplicated, warm without being unprofessional. The goal is for a visitor to land on the website and immediately feel that this is a trustworthy, quality property.

Our design principles for this project:

  • Visual-first layout — large, beautiful room and facility photography as the hero
  • Clean typography — easy to read, professional font pairings
  • Mobile-first — designed for phones first, then scaled up to desktop
  • Intuitive navigation — guests find what they need in seconds
  • Clear calls-to-action — "Book Now" and "Contact Us" buttons placed strategically
  • Fast performance — optimized images and clean code for quick load times
